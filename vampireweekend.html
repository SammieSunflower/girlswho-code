About this group:

Vampire Weekend is an American rock band from New York City. They were formed in 2006 and they are currently signed to Columbia Records. The band's members include lead vocalist, guitarist Ezra Koenig, multi-instrumentalist Rostam Batmanglij, drummer Chris Tomson, and bassist Chris Baio.
